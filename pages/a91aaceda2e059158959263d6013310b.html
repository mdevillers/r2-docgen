List, send, get, set, signal handlers of child

Usage: dk Signal commands
| dk List all signal handlers of child process
| dk Send KILL signal to child
| dk =1 Set signal handler for in child
| dk? Name/signum resolver
| dko[?] Reset skip or cont options for given signal
| dko [|skip|cont] On signal SKIP handler or CONT into
| dkj List all signal handlers in JSON
